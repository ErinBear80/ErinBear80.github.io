Travel Vocabulary

Here are some basic vocabulary words you should know in French.

  • Hello = Bonjour
  • How are you? = Comment allez-vous?
  • I am well. = Je vais bien.
  • Where is the train station? = Où est la gare?
  • Where is the bathroom? = Où est la toilette?

The Verb "to have"

In French, each verb has many forms depending on who is the subject of the sentence. Here are the forms of the verb "to have".

  • I have = J'ai
  • You have (informal) = Tu as
  • He has = Il a
  • She has = Elle a
  • We have = Nous avons
  • You have (formal) = Vous avez
  • They have (males only, or mixed group) = Ils ont
  • They have (females only) = Elles ont

The Verb "to be"

  • I am = Je suis
  • You are (informal) = Tu es
  • He is = Il est
  • She is = Elle est
  • We are = Nous sommes
  • You are (formal) = Vous êtes
  • They are (males only, or mixed group) = Ils sont
  • They are (females only) = Elles sont
